03 — How PH Works

The Mechanics of Product Hunt

🔄

Daily Reset

Every product starts at zero. The clock resets at 12:01am PST — you compete only with that day's launches.

⚡

Velocity Signals

Early upvote speed matters more than raw totals. A strong first-hour burst tells the algorithm you're worth surfacing.

💬

Comment Quality

Thoughtful comments outweigh numbers. Engagement depth is a ranking signal — drive conversations, not just votes.

Key insight: The algorithm rewards genuine engagement. Manufactured upvote spikes are detected and discounted — sometimes penalized.

06 — Name, Tagline, Thumbnail

Your First Impression in the Feed

🏷

Tagline Rules

Benefit-led, not feature-led. Under 60 characters. Answers: "What do I get?" — not "What is it?"

🖼

Thumbnail Rules

Bold, high-contrast, readable at small size. Test at 60×60px. No walls of text. One clear visual hook.

Tagline Examples
✗ Feature-led (weak)
"AI-powered document management platform"
✓ Benefit-led (strong)
"Find any doc in 5 seconds — guaranteed"
Character limit: 60 chars max
07 — Gallery & Demo Video

Show, Don't Tell

Demo Video
  • Under 60 seconds — attention drops fast
  • Lead with the "aha" moment in the first 10 seconds
  • Skip corporate intros, logo animations, and backstory
  • Show the product actually doing its job
Gallery Images
  • Minimum 4–6 images — walk through the core workflow
  • Each image should stand alone — many users skip the video
  • Use clean product screenshots + context captions
  • Final image: a social proof or key stat if you have one
Structure your video: Problem (5s) → Product doing the job (40s) → Result / CTA (15s). That's it.
08 — Maker's First Comment

Your First Comment Sets the Tone

  • Post immediately at launch — be the first comment
  • Tell a personal story about why you built it
  • Invite feedback, don't pitch — ask a genuine question
  • Converts browsers into upvoters and turns voters into commenters
✗ Pitch: "We're the best tool for X — check us out and upvote if you agree!"
✓ Story: "I built this after losing 3 hours to [problem]. Curious — does this resonate with you?"
Anatomy of a Great First Comment
1
The pain: One sentence on the problem you faced personally
2
The insight: What made you realize the existing solution was broken
3
The ask: An open question that invites a real reply
09 — Hunter or No Hunter

Do You Need a Hunter?

🙋

Self-Submit

Completely valid in 2024+. PH reduced hunter algorithm weight. Your own network and listing quality matter more. No dependencies, full control.

✓ Recommended for most founders
🎯

Use a Hunter

Helpful if they have 10K+ PH followers and are genuinely excited about your product. Their audience must be relevant to yours.

Contact them 2+ weeks in advance
  • If using a hunter — pitch them clearly: who it's for, what problem it solves, traction you have
  • Never pay for a hunter — violates PH rules and signals inauthenticity
  • Most top hunters like @rrhoover founders now prioritize founders who submit themselves
10 — Build Your Audience

Build Before You Launch

PH Upcoming Page
  • Activates 2–3 weeks before launch — get yours live immediately
  • Visitors click "Notify me" → PH emails them at launch
  • Aim for 100+ notify-me signups before launch day
  • Warm traffic converts 3–5× better than cold outreach
Warm Your Channels
  • Twitter/X & LinkedIn: post about the problem you solve, 2× per week
  • Newsletter: tease the launch one week out
  • Communities: Slack, Discord, Reddit — add value first, pitch last
  • Your audience should feel like launch is the natural next step
The notify-me list is your most valuable pre-launch asset. These are people who asked to be told when you launch — treat them accordingly.

12 — First-Hour Push

The First Hour is the Algorithm Test

First Hour (12:01–1am)
  • Alert your 5–15 closest supporters — people who will genuinely engage
  • These are friends, customers, advisors — not random followers
  • Ask them to comment, not just vote — comments amplify the signal
  • Post your maker first comment immediately
Rest of Day (1am–midnight)
  • Stagger outreach — email, Twitter, newsletter, communities at different times
  • Front-loading everything creates a spike then silence — bad signal
  • Post updates on your launch progress to re-engage your network
  • Keep responding to every comment throughout the day
Velocity > Volume. 20 genuine upvotes in the first hour is worth more than 100 votes at 6pm.
13 — Ask Without Breaking Rules

How to Ask Without Getting Flagged

✗ Banned Language
"Please upvote us on Product Hunt!"
"Help us get #1 — every upvote counts!"
"Vote for us so we can win today"
"If you like this, make sure to upvote"
✓ Compliant Language
"We just launched on Product Hunt today"
"We're live — here's the link if you want to check it out"
"Would love your thoughts on our PH listing"
"We launched [product] — feedback welcome!"
  • PH detects patterns in outreach — vote rings are penalized, not just ignored
  • Informative framing converts as well or better — it feels like news, not a pitch
14 — Comments Are the Conversation

Comments Are Where Trust Compounds

  • Reply to every comment within the hour — speed signals you're present
  • Be human and generous — thank critics, not just fans
  • Add substance — a one-word reply is worse than no reply
  • Comment threads are live customer interviews — mine them
Engagement depth is a direct ranking signal. A 10-comment thread beats 50 upvotes in the algorithm.
Response Formula
Praise
Thank them + ask what specific feature stood out
Criticism
Acknowledge it genuinely + share what's on the roadmap
Question
Answer fully + invite them to try it and share back
Feature request
"Adding this to the list — thanks for the detail"
15 — Post-Launch

The Launch Doesn't End at Midnight

First 48 Hours
  • Thank your supporters publicly — a tweet, a LinkedIn post, an email
  • Capture visitor emails — add a targeted offer for PH traffic on your homepage
  • Share results transparently — your ranking, signups, feedback received
  • Respond to any late comments — PH traffic trickles for 48+ hours
Long Tail (Weeks & Months)
  • Embed the PH badge on your homepage and landing pages
  • The DA 91 backlink is permanent SEO value — keep your listing accurate
  • Repurpose positive PH comments as testimonials
  • Use your PH ranking in investor decks and press pitches
"Launched and disappeared" is the most common post-launch mistake. The follow-through is where most of the value is extracted.
